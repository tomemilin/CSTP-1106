    2. Soccer
      3. Global sport

      As mentioned earlier, Soccer is a global sport which implies that it is viewed and played across different countries. Unlike other sports, soccer is the only sport which is enjoyed by people of all age group. It has bound the people of different race and community together.

      Easy to play

      Another reason behind the growing popularity of soccer is due to its simplicity. The basics of the sport are easy to understand which is why even kids love to play it. Even if one doesn’t have a thorough knowledge of its rules, anyone can understand the basics by watching it for the first time. It’s less complex nature has made it reached beyond the countries boundaries.

      Artful Techniques

      Controlling the ball on the ground requires a lot of grace and artful techniques. The players have to tackle the opponent players while keeping the ball moving from one corner of the groun d to the opponent’s end. The players have to practice hours and hours to learn all the techniques which can help them in becoming a future star.
